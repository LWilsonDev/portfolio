Beethoven Music Memory

Having previously built a little memory game to help children learn to differentiate between musical pitches, I was approached by a music teacher in Germany to extend and adapt the game for his students, to help them with their studies of Beethoven Symphony No.1. The game is built using JavaScript/JQuery and is available in German and English. The user flips 2 tiles and listens to the Beethoven motifs to match pairs. There are 2 levels - the harder level removes the notation and users have to just use their ears to find the matching motifs.
