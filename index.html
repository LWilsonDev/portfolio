The Beer List

This is a Django app where users can log in securely, keep track of their favourite beers, and browse other user's profiles for inspiration. With the growing trend for craft beer and independent breweries, I wanted to create an app where users can review the beers they have tried, and keep track of their favourites.

Space API's

This is the start of my exploration into NASA's API's. This is a Django project built using Python, utilising AJAX to fetch real-time data about the International Space Station. You can find the current position of the ISS above the Earth, and see the location on Google Maps, as well as find out how many people are currently in space
